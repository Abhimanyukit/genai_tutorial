ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Human Resource Policy

1. Working Hours
- Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Employees are expected to complete 40 working hours per week.
- Flexible working hours are available with manager approval.

2. Attendance
- Employees must mark attendance through the HR portal.
- Late arrival beyond 15 minutes more than three times in a month may require manager approval.

3. Remote Work
- Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week.
- Full-time remote work requires department head approval.
- Employees must maintain a stable internet connection during working hours.

4. Dress Code
- Business casual attire is expected from Monday to Thursday.
- Casual attire is allowed on Fridays.

5. Performance Review
- Performance reviews are conducted twice a year.
- Employees are evaluated based on project delivery, teamwork, communication, and technical skills.

6. Code of Conduct
- Employees must maintain professional behavior.
- Company information must remain confidential.
- Harassment and discrimination are strictly prohibited.

7. Office Facilities
- Free Wi-Fi is available.
- Cafeteria operates from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
- Parking is provided for employees with valid parking permits.
