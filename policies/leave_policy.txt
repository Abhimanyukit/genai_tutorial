ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Leave Policy

1. Casual Leave
- Every employee receives 12 Casual Leaves per calendar year.
- Casual Leave cannot be carried forward.

2. Sick Leave
- Every employee receives 15 Sick Leaves annually.
- Medical certificates are required if sick leave exceeds 3 consecutive days.

3. Earned Leave
- Employees receive 20 Earned Leaves every year.
- Up to 10 Earned Leaves can be carried forward to the next calendar year.

4. Maternity Leave
- Eligible employees receive 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.

5. Paternity Leave
- Eligible employees receive 10 working days of paid paternity leave.

6. Public Holidays
- Employees receive all company-declared public holidays.
- Holiday calendars are published every January.

7. Leave Approval
- Leave requests should be submitted at least 3 working days in advance.
- Emergency leave should be communicated to the reporting manager immediately.

8. Leave Encashment
- Employees may encash unused Earned Leave during separation according to company policy.
