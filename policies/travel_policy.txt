ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Travel and Expense Policy

1. Air Travel
- Economy class should be booked for domestic travel.
- Business class requires Vice President approval.

2. Hotel Accommodation
- Hotel reimbursement is limited to ₹5,000 per night for domestic travel.
- Higher limits require prior approval.

3. Local Transportation
- Taxi expenses are reimbursable.
- Ride-sharing services such as Uber and Ola are allowed.
- Public transportation should be preferred whenever practical.

4. Meals
- Meal reimbursement limit is ₹1,500 per day.
- Alcohol expenses are not reimbursable.

5. Travel Approval
- All business travel must be approved before booking.
- International travel requires department head approval.

6. Expense Claims
- Expense reports must be submitted within 7 days after travel completion.
- Original receipts must be attached for all expenses above ₹500.

7. Personal Expenses
- Personal shopping, entertainment, and sightseeing expenses are not reimbursable.

8. International Travel
- Employees must have valid passports and visas before travel.
- Travel insurance is provided by the company for approved international trips.
